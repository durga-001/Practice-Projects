Lasagna Recipe

Preparing the vegetable balls takes time, as the vegetables need to be finely chopped or shredded, mixed with flours and seasonings, shaped, and fried. Using a vegetable chopper helps speed up the prep and gives a more even texture. The vegetable balls can also be made ahead, fried in advance, and refrigerated, which reduces last-minute work. The sauce is flexible and easy to adjust. You can add a little water or vegetable stock for a thinner gravy, or use a small amount of cornstarch slurry for a thicker consistency.

  • finely chopped red cabbage
  • grated carrot
  • finely chopped capsicum (green bell pepper)
  • finely chopped French beans
  • finely chopped spring onions
  1. Cut the vegetables
  2. Fry in oil
  3. Add mirch masala salt as per taste
  4. Add manchurain balls
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes
